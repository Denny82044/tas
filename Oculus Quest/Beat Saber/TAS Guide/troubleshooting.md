# Troubleshooting
This is a list of known issues and fixes for any bugs in the TASsing process.
## Why are the notes going so fast in the replay?
This is usually caused by JDFixer, with the easiest fix being to go to the JDFixer menu, and turning on the "Disable Mod" switch until you need it next (image below).
![JDFixerDisableMod](https://github.com/Denny82044/tas/blob/main/Oculus%20Quest/Beat%20Saber/TAS%20Guide/JDFixerDisableMod.png)
